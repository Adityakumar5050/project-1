Mathura Vrindavan Govardhan

It is true that Mathura is the birthplace of Lord Krishna, the incarnation of
Lord Vishnu.Traditionally it is also recognized as the capital of Braj Bhoomi.
Mathura is located in Uttar Pradesh and is located at a distance of 145 km
southeast of Delhi and the northwestern part of Agra at a distance of 58 km
into two parts, one part occupying places like Mahavan, Baldeo, Mat and
Bajna and the western part of Yamuna that covers the Mathura region
encompassing Vrindavan, Govardhan, Kusum Sarovar, Barsana and Nandgaon..

Radharani, this is the place where it had happened.she's seen as equal
to Krishna, the other half of his soul split in two a long time before,
and finally coming back to each other. Their love was inevitable,even
